About
The pre-sales partner AEs request by name.

Kevin McGinnity is a Senior AI Solutions Engineer with 7+ years closing enterprise SaaS deals across 135+ logos and $7M+ in ARR, recognized as Team MVP six consecutive years at Oracle NetSuite.

Kevin is a problem solver, and his favorite part of the job is discovery. Not because of what he's selling, but because of what he gets to learn. Every new prospect is a different business, a different set of problems, a different way of operating. A decade of industry experience has taught him how to get past the surface answers and find the real ones: the north stars a company is quietly chasing, the bottlenecks nobody put in the slide deck, the reason the busy CEO actually said yes to this conversation.

Everyone in SaaS is talking about AI. Fewer people are actually using it in their daily workflow in a way that matters. Kevin runs a live Claude and NetSuite MCP integration in his day-to-day. It has changed how he preps, how he demos, and how fast he can tailor a solution to a specific prospect. Seven Anthropic certifications in, the most valuable thing he has learned is that the tool is only as good as the questions you know how to ask. Turns out discovery skills transfer.

In six years at NetSuite he has consulted 475+ companies, closed 135+ logos totaling $7M+ in ARR, and earned Team MVP recognition every fiscal year.
